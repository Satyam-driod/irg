Welcome To Our College

Government Polytechnic, Saharsa is a AICTE approved technical institute, established under the Government of Bihar. G.P.Saharsa is one of the best polytechnics in Bihar since 1966 and produced many technocrats and alumni from the all part of the state in service for society and contribution for the Nation Building.

Our Courses

MS Office Certificate Programme

This is a 3-month or 6-month programme where the students will learn about the top-used and prominent applications of Microsoft Office like MS PowerPoint, MS Access, MS Excel, MS Word. Students learn how to use these applications on a regular basis.

View Details

Certificate/Diploma in Programming Languages

Programming languages are the languages of a computer such as Python, JAVA, C++, C, MySQL etc. After obtaining a certificate or diploma in a programming language, the students have an option to go for a detailed course as this short-term course only teaches basic things.

View Details

Computer-Aided Design and Drawing (CADD)

Students with a technical background can opt for this Computer-Aided Design and Drawing short-term course. The students learn about various CAD programmes and Software’s like Infraworks, Fusion360, AutoCAD etc.

View Details

Web Design and Development

This short-term course can be completed in both 3 months or 6 months where the students learn about how to create and maintain e-Commerce sites, blogs and websites. Topics like multimedia and web scripting & graphics are the dominant topics in this course.

View Details

Multimedia and Animation

Short-term course in multimedia and animation has become one of the most favoured courses among today’s youth. In the course, the students learn a thing or two about Multimedia Design, Game Design & Animation, Film Design & Animation, Basics of Animation, VFX and VFX Pro.

View Details

Digital Marketing

By pursuing a short-term course in digital marketing, the students get to cover a wide range of topics like Brand Management, SMO, A/B Testing, Analytics, Lead Generation, Affiliate Marketing, Email Marketing, Social Media, Content Marketing & Writing and SEO.

View Details
